GetScrumban Game Intro

Scrum is an incredibly simple, effective and highly-popular software development framework. Despite its simplicity, Scrum can be difficult to master.

Scrumban is also a simple framework, but is widely misunderstood. Some believe it to be a new software development framework that combines “the best” elements of Scrum and Kanban. Others believe it to be using kanban systems inside of Scrum. Others have yet different understandings.

Scrumban is an easy-to-use framework of complementary tools and capabilities for Scrum. It can be used to accelerate new adoptions, to help overcome common pitfalls & challenges, or to evolve a new framework that sustains value-delivery and agility over time.

The GetScrumban game lets you experience how Scrumban can help amplify capabilities, overcome common challenges, and forge alternative paths to agility.

The Get Scrumban Game

The Get Scrumban Game simulates a software development team that adopts Scrumban principles to deliver higher quality software more predictably. While playing, you will experience common problems that software development teams have and see first-hand techniques that can be used to solve them.

Some of the lessons you will learn:

Visualize Value Streams

Learn different ways of visualizing how your work gets done and how to easily show stakeholders progress.

Classes of Service

See how defining policies around classes of service can help your development team be flexible to changing business needs.

Continuous Flow

Learn when Continuous Flow can help a team be more productive than a traditional fixed iteration, and see how to visualize metrics based on that flow.

Pull instead of Push

See what happens when your teams start pulling work to do instead of having it pushed upon them.

why play this Game?

The GetScrumban Game allows players to experience how Scrumban brings new perspective & capabilities to a Scrum context.

Scrum Team members & trainers / coaches will benefit most, but managers & execs also gain perspective from an improved understanding of the framework.

This game is an ideal aid for quickly ramping up new teams and orienting new employees to the key principles underlying Scrumban.

Individuals don’t have to be co-located to play with and against one another during the SAME game sessions.
